Commercial Licensing, Support

Pivotal provides a full range of commercial support and services for RabbitMQ.

Pivotal offers commercial support agreements for the most recent open source release of RabbitMQ.

We also produce a commercially licensed release of RabbitMQ.This includes all of the features of the open source version and contains additional commercially focussed components. Support agreements are also available for the commercial release.

Training

We offer on-line and instructor-led training courses worldwide that cover learning messaging with RabbitMQ, use cases, patterns, best practice design and architecture.

Consulting

Our professional consulting services are highly flexible to address specific issues such as on-site troubleshooting, design review, deployment planning, project delivery and other value-adding services tailored to suit your needs.

Please contact us for more information.
