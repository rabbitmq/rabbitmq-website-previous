Pivotal provides a full range of commercial support and services for RabbitMQ.

Commercial Licensing, Support, Training and Consulting

Pivotal offers commercial support agreements for the most recent open source release of RabbitMQ.

We also produce a commercially licensed release of RabbitMQ under the vFabric™ brand. This includes all of the features of the open source version and contains additional commercially focussed components. Support agreements are also available from Pivotal for vFabric RabbitMQ.

We offer on-line and instructor-led training courses worldwide that cover learning messaging with AMQP, use cases and patterns with RabbitMQ and best practice design and architecture.

Our professional consulting services are highly flexible to address specific issues such as on-site troubleshooting, design review, deployment planning, project delivery and other value-adding services tailored to suit your needs.
